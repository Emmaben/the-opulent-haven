Welcome To The Opulent Haven

Perched on the 11th floor of the iconic The Opulent Haven Edinburgh Hotel, The Opulent Haven Lounge immerses guests in the vibrant spirit of Scotland’s capital. Offering a curated selection of Gaelic cuisines, all-day dining, and direct access to the rooftop The Opulent Haven Deck, visitors enjoy breathtaking 360-degree views of Edinburgh. Whether for a meal, a drink, or to unwind, The Opulent Haven Lounge offers an unforgettable experience.
